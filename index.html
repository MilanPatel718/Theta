Who are we?

Sigma Beta Rho is a premier nationwide multicultural fraternity based upon the principles of Society, Brotherhood, and Remembrance.

Established nationally on August 16th, 1996 at the University of Pennsylvania, Sigma Beta Rho is one of the fastest growing nationwide networks.

Our pillars are Society, Brotherhood, and Remembrance

Our colors are Red, Black, and Green

Our motto is "Brotherhood Beyond All Barriers"

Our Mission Statement is "To cultivate a global leadership network, founded in brotherhood, and impassioned by service to each other and our world."
